About Our Mission

Mapping toilets affected by long rains.

Our Mission

Our mission is to identify and map toilets that have been adversely affected by prolonged rainfall. These conditions cause toilets to collapse or become filled with water, posing significant health and safety risks to communities.

By mapping these affected toilets, we aim to raise awareness of the dangers posed by compromised sanitation facilities and provide data that can help in taking corrective measures to ensure safe and sanitary conditions for all.

We are committed to working towards a safer environment by identifying these risks and advocating for better sanitation solutions to mitigate the impact of climate change on vulnerable communities.
